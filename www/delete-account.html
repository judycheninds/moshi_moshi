Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your Moshi Moshi account and all associated data at any time.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

How to Delete Your Account In-App

  1. Open the Moshi Moshi app on your device
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap Account
  4. Select Delete Account
  5. Confirm your identity and tap Confirm Delete

Request Deletion by Email

You can also request account deletion by emailing us with your registered email address.

Email: support@moshimoshicall.com

We will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

What Data Gets Deleted

  • Your profile information (name, email address, phone number)
  • All reservation history and call logs
  • Payment information and billing history
  • App preferences and settings

Some anonymized data may be retained for legal compliance per our Privacy Policy.
