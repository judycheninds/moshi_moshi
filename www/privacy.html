Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide securely, including your name, email address, phone number, and encrypted payment information used to process service fees.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly to provide and improve the MoshiMoshi concierge telephony service. Your phone number is used to relay booking outcomes via SMS, and your payment details are securely tokenized using Stripe. We do NOT store your credit card information directly on our servers.

3. AI Processing

By using the platform, you acknowledge that conversations with restaurants are processed using artificial intelligence models. Transcripts are temporarily retained strictly to evaluate reservation success and troubleshoot failures.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share or sell your personal information. We only share necessary reservation details (Names and Phone Numbers) with the restaurant being booked.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@moshimoshi.com.
